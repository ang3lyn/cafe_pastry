COLD BREW LATTE

Cold brew coffee, milk, simple syrup, and ice are needed to make this caffeinated drink. Cold brew coffee takes at least 8 hours to make, so be sure to make that first. The trick with layering is sugar and ice. The bottom layer must be sweeter than the top layer. Ice helps to keep the layers separated. Easy!
