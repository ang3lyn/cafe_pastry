ICED AMERICANO

An Iced Americano is a cold drink made with ice, espresso, and chilled water. It’s the iced version of an Americano, which is made with just espresso and hot water. The drink must be made with espresso to be called an Iced Americano. If it’s made with any other kind of coffee, it’s no longer an Iced Americano. Espresso should be made in an espresso machine or a Nespresso. Iced Americano is a watered down espresso. It’s similar to strong brewed coffee made in a drip coffee machine.
